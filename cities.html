My Favorite Cities

  1. Denver, CO

    Denver is the capital and largest city of the state of Colorado, known for its picturesque mountain views and outdoor activities.

    Interesting Fact: Denver is one of the sunniest cities in the United States, with about 300 days of sunshine per year.

  2. Albany, NY

    Albany is the capital city of the state of New York, rich in history and culture.

    Interesting Fact: Albany is one of the oldest surviving European settlements from the original thirteen colonies and the longest continuously chartered city in the United States.

  3. Kunshan

    Kunshan is a county-level city in Jiangsu Province, China, known for its beautiful gardens and canals.

    Interesting Fact: Kunshan is often referred to as the "Venice of the East" due to its scenic waterways and bridges.
