Huangshan

Huangshan, known for its breathtaking mountain scenery, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Famous for its granite peaks, pine trees, and hot springs, it is a popular destination for tourists and photographers.

Shanghai

Shanghai, a global financial hub, blends modern skyscrapers with historic architecture. The Bund, with its colonial-era buildings, contrasts with the futuristic skyline of Pudong across the Huangpu River.

Los Angeles

Los Angeles, the entertainment capital of the world, is known for its Mediterranean climate, ethnic diversity, and the film and television industry. Iconic attractions include Hollywood, Beverly Hills, and Venice Beach.

Denver

Denver, the Mile-High City, is known for its vibrant cultural scene and proximity to the Rocky Mountains. It's a gateway for outdoor activities like skiing and hiking, and boasts a lively downtown area with museums and breweries.
