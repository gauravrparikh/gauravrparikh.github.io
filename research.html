Research Statement

I am broadly interested in deriving insights from complex datasets including tabular, geospatial and textual data, with lots of missingness specifically arising from applications in governemnt and healthcare. Specifically, I am interested in observation data where establishing causality is hard. I have worked on transportation data, crime data and more recently I am working on electronic healthcare records of patients in critical care. I deeply care about addressing social, ethical, political and environmental problems through the power of data science.
